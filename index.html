Questions Clients Ask

Everything to know before starting.

How long does a home interior project take?

Timeline depends on scope and site readiness. A modular kitchen may take a few weeks, while full home interiors usually need a planned execution schedule.

Do you provide 3D designs?

Yes. Design visualization can be included based on the project scope so you can understand the look before execution.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes. We discuss priority areas, materials and finish levels to create a practical plan for your budget.

Do you handle materials and execution?

Yes. Castle Design offers turnkey support including design, material planning, vendor coordination and site execution.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Chennai and nearby areas. Share your location on WhatsApp to confirm availability.

Do you provide warranty?

Warranty depends on selected materials, hardware and project scope. Details should be confirmed in the final quotation.
